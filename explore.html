Field Trips

Field trips , both in-person and online ,make the indian botanic garden and extesion of your classroom,The garden offers instructor-led field trips for grades pre-k-12, as well as guidance and on-site activity options for self-guided exporation.

  • Understand Field Trip Logistics

    • Expectations

  • Teachers and chaperones are responsible for ensuring the group's appropriate behavior throughout the
          entire visit
  • Students must be accompained by an adult at all times. We recommend you break into small groups ,
           With one adault per every six to eight students.
  • Please have your students stay on the paths at all times. This is run in our Conservatory and while
           visiting our outdoor gardens
  • Please stagger your visits to the restrooms to avoid overcrowding.

    • Lunch Areas

  • Groups are not permitted to eat in the conservatory.
  • Storage space for lunches is not available.
  • Your group is welcome to eat in our outdoor garden spaces. if eating in these spaces. please bring trash bags to "Pack-out"yuor waste. Please remember that outdoor       gardens are part of our museum. Please remind students to remain on the paths at all times.

    • Accessibility
